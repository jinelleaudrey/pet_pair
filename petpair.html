T H E   I S S U E

Animals all over the world lack warm homes to live in, especially dogs and cats. People usually adopt puppies/kittens and disregard the older dogs/cats. But if any animal doesn't get adopted, they end up spending the rest of their lives in a cage and eventually be put down.

Approximately 6.5 million companion animals enter U.S. animal shelters nationwide every year. Of those, approximately 3.3 million are dogs and 3.2 million are cats. Approximately 3.2 million shelter animals are adopted each year (1.6 million dogs and 1.6 million cats).

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to get as many dogs and cats, regardless of age or breed, to have loving owners! Through our comprehensive questionnaire that asks about your interests and personality, we will match you up with a dog or cat that will most likely get along with you.
